Mac and Cheese

Mac and Cheese for the win!

Use this simple recipe to create award winning mac and cheese. You can't go wrong with cheesy noodles! Let's eat!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Butter
  1. Boil noodles.
  2. Melt cheese.
  3. Mix noodles, cheese, milk, and butter.
  4. Enjoy.
