Lasagna

Lasagna for the win!

Use this simple recipe to create an award winning lasagna. You can't go wrong with layers of meat, cheese, sauce, and noodle! Let's eat!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  1. Cook meat.
  2. Boil noodles.
  3. Layer as follows:
    1. Meat
    2. Sauce
    3. Noodle
    4. Cheese
    5. Repeat
  4. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
  5. Cool for 10 minutes and enjoy.
